Pricing

What this costs you

FREE always and forever

This is a personal project. I am a 15-year-old photographer and I shoot vintage vehicles purely because I love them on film.

There is nothing to pay. But if you want to support the work, there are two things that actually help.

Film

35mm film is not cheap. If you would like to buy me a roll or two, Kodak or anything you can find, I will shoot your vehicle on it and every frame is yours to keep.

Connections

You already know the community. If you know another owner with a vehicle worth documenting, send them this way. The more the better.
